Ivy

my hands
my feet
your love
so sweet
if i could stop it
you know i would

temptation grows
you know i know
how this ends
so sad, my friend
if i could stop it
you know i would

but i'm in too deep
no time to sweep
away the ivy
that grows beneath
this lively feeling
of your discreet
vines of feelings
that spread the rash

tainted upon this severed path
of bittersweet
ivy beneath
this freckled love
this push and shove
it scares me so

i run and i run

- written by: patrick o'hara
